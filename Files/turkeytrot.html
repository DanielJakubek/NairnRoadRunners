Turkey Trot


26 December 2024

Start and finish at the Maggot Sports Ground in Nairn. It is a great way to burn off all those extra calories from Christmas Day. Registration and refreshments are at the Nairn Seamans Hall. Register from 10:00am to 10:45am. Race starts 11:15am. This is a predicted time run and no timekeeping devices are to be used - This includes fancy "where am I now? GPS Gadgets". Parking available at Dunbar Golf Course and Maggot carpark.

No watches!

The course is 5k and runners guess their finish time- no watches allowed!
