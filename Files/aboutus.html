Who are we?

We are a small friendly club catering for all running abilities in the coastal town of Nairn. Whether you are interested in taking up running, a bit of a novice or an experienced runner we invite you to join us on any of our club sessions. We have coaches that run the sessions and can give you advice on your running. We organize races throughout the year from 5k, 10k, half marathon and cross country. We have runners competing in all of these as well as marathon, Ultras and Hill races.
